Objectives

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Graph vectors on the \(xy\)-plane.
  • Express a vector in component form and \(\bf{ij}\) form.
  • Find the magnitude and direction of a vector.
  • Perform basic operations (addition, subtraction, and scalar multiplication) with vectors.
  • Solve applications involving bearings using vectors.
