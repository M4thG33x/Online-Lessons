Sum & Difference Identities

For any angles or values \(A\) and \(B\), the following relationships are always true.

\[\begin{align*} \sin(A + B) &= \sin(A)\cos(B) + \cos(A)\sin(B) \\[4pt] \sin(A - B) &= \sin(A)\cos(B) - \cos(A)\sin(B) \\[16pt] \cos(A + B) &= \cos(A)\cos(B) - \sin(A)\sin(B) \\[4pt] \cos(A - B) &= \cos(A)\cos(B) + \sin(A)\sin(B) \\[16pt] \tan(A + B) &= \frac{\tan(A) + \tan(B)}{1 - \tan(A)\tan(B)} \\[4pt] \tan(A - B) &= \frac{\tan(A) - \tan(B)}{1 + \tan(A)\tan(B)} \end{align*}\]
