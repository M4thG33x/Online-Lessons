Objectives

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Sketch the graph of a parabola, ellipse, or hyperbola given a conic equation in polar form.
  • Given a graph or other information about a conic with a focus at the origin, write a corresponding equation in polar form.
  • Identify the eccentricity, type of conic, and directrix for a conic with a focus at the origin given in polar form.
