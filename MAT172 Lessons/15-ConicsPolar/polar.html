Conic Sections

The term conic section refers to the geometric shapes that are generated by the cross-section formed when intersecting a plane with a cone. The four primary shapes that we will learn about are circles, ellipses, parabolas, and hyperbolas. In addition, there are a few more shapes, which are called degenerate conics, that include a line, a pair of intersecting lines, or a single point. Our focus in this course will be on the primary shapes.
