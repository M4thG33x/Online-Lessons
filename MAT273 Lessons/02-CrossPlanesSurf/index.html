Objectives

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Compute the cross product of two vectors.
  • Compute the projection of one vector onto another.
  • Solve applications involving the dot and cross products.
  • Write the vector equation of a line in \(\mathbb{R}^3\).
  • Write the equation of a plane.
  • Identify surfaces and write their equations.
