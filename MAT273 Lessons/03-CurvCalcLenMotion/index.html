Curves, Calculus, Length & Curvature

Vector Functions

Objectives

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Graph vector-valued functions.
  • Evaluate limits of vector-valued functions.
  • Determine derivatives of vector-valued functions.
  • Evaluate integrals of vector-valued functions.
  • Determine the arc length of curves defined by vector-valued functions.
  • Rewrite vector-valued functions using arc length as a parameter.
  • Determine the unit Tangent, unit Normal and unit Binormal vectors.
  • Determine the Curvature and Torsion of a curve.
  • Determine vector functions for position, velocity and acceleration.
  • Solve applications involving projectile motion.
  • Determine the Tangential and Normal components of Acceleration
